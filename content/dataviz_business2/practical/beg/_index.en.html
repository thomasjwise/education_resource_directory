Section 2: Specifying the axis’s


Exercise 4: Using an alternative to theme, increase the limits of the x & y axis. Hint: Use lims(y = c(), x = c()), placing the limits at 0 & 100

  ggplot() + 
    geom_point(data = WDB_1999, 
               mapping = aes(x = lifeexp.ma, 
                             y = lifeexp.fe, 
                             colour = Continent)) + 
    theme_minimal() +
